About The Site

Welcome to Geo-Cannon! Our website allows you to dynamically and efficiently chat with other Geo-Cannon users! Simply post a message to show your location and find other user that are around you to chat with them. Chatting local made simple. The Website is a product of the squirtle-crew Team of the UCI Coding Boot Camp! Our team consists of Josiah De La Torre, https://github.com/jodelat, Andrew Nguyen, https://github.com/andrewnguyen14, Viet Nguyen, https://github.com/BuiltbyViet, and Michael Mamich, https://github.com/MMMamich.

Why The Turtles?

Whats up with all the turtles man?!? Well when group projects started for our cohort, team names had to be based on a particular npm package that was available. We decided that that we would make our own npm package called squirtle-crew and thus our turtle origin! Go download our npm package at https://www.npmjs.com/package/squirtle-crew; 271 people already have despite the readme clearly saying that the package does nothing! Oh and by the way Geo-Cannon is a pun of a pokemon move called hydro cannon, however I'm not going to tell you which pokemon knows that move...
